Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does a demolition project take? +
Most residential jobs are done in 1–3 days. Full structure teardowns typically take 1–2 days; concrete removal and pool demo are often completed same-day depending on scope.
Do you pull permits for demolition? +
Yes. We handle all required permits and coordinate utility disconnections before any work begins, keeping your project fully compliant with local regulations.
Do you remove debris and haul it away? +
Every job includes complete debris removal and site cleanup. We load, haul, and dispose of all material — leaving your property clean and ready for what's next.
Can you do partial or selective demolition? +
Absolutely. We specialize in selective demo — removing specific walls, additions, or structures while leaving the rest of your property fully intact and undamaged.
How much does demolition cost? +
Every project is different. We offer free, no-obligation estimates so you know the number before anything starts. Call Mike directly at (239) 848-6143 for a fast quote.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes. Willpower Solutions is fully licensed and insured for all demolition and site work in Southwest Florida. We carry full liability coverage on every job.
What happens after a pool is demolished? +
After demolition the pool is filled, compacted, and graded to solid level ground. The result is stable, buildable land ready for a patio, landscaping, or new construction.
Do you handle emergency or same-day jobs? +
Yes. We're available 24/7 for storm damage, structural failures, and emergency teardowns. Call us any time — we respond fast and show up ready to work.
